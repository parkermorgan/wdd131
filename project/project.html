Welcome to CloneCollective!

Welcome to CloneCollective, your ultimate resource dedicated to the elite soldiers of the Star Wars universe: clone troopers. Whether you're a die-hard fan of The Clone Wars series or captivated by the latest adventures in The Bad Batch, CloneCollective is your comprehensive guide. Dive into detailed profiles of iconic clones like Captain Rex, Commander Cody, and Echo, exploring their unique traits, battle strategies, and pivotal moments in the galactic saga. Stay updated with the latest news, fan theories, and discussions surrounding these legendary soldiers who shaped the fate of the galaxy. Join our community of enthusiasts to celebrate the enduring legacy of clone troopers and their ongoing impact in the Star Wars universe.

Notable Characters

Captain Rex (CT-7567)

Series: The Clone Wars, The Bad Batch, Star Wars Rebels, Ahsoka

Role: Rex is one of the most prominent and beloved clones in the series. He serves as a loyal captain under Anakin Skywalker and later Ahsoka Tano. Rex is known for his strong sense of duty and moral integrity. He plays a significant role during Order 66 and helps Ahsoka escape.

Commander Cody (CT-2224)

Series: The Clone Wars, The Bad Batch, Revenge of the Sith

Role: Cody is a high-ranking clone who serves under Obi-Wan Kenobi. He is known for his leadership and combat skills. Cody is also notable for his involvement in the execution of Order 66, where he turns against Obi-Wan.

Fives (CT-5555)

Series: The Clone Wars

Role: Fives is part of the Domino Squad and later becomes an ARC trooper. He is central to the plotline involving the discovery of the inhibitor chips implanted in clones to ensure their compliance with Order 66. His investigation into the chips ultimately leads to his tragic death.

Echo (CT-1409)

Series: The Clone Wars, The Bad Batch

Role: Initially part of the Domino Squad, Echo is presumed dead but is later found alive and heavily modified by the Separatists. He joins Clone Force 99 (The Bad Batch) and becomes an essential member due to his enhanced abilities and knowledge.

Hunter (CT-9901)

Series: The Clone Wars, The Bad Batch

Role: Hunter is the leader of Clone Force 99, a group of clones with enhanced traits. He has enhanced sensory abilities, making him an excellent tracker. Hunter is a central character in "The Bad Batch," leading his team through various missions and navigating the complexities of the post-Clone Wars galaxy.

Crosshair (CT-9904)

Series: The Clone Wars, The Bad Batch

Role: Crosshair is a member of Clone Force 99 with enhanced marksmanship skills. His loyalty to the Empire and conflict with his former squadmates create a compelling and dramatic storyline in "The Bad Batch." His character explores themes of loyalty and identity.
